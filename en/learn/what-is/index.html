What is XiaoBenBen?

XiaoBenBen is a local Windows player-notes utility that helps League of Legends players remember people from real matches and the private notes they wrote about them.

Updated: 2026-07-10

Who it is for

It is for players who regularly play ranked, normal or ARAM matches and want context when a previous teammate or opponent appears again.

Core functions

It records the current roster, lets you add positive, negative and text notes, and exposes matching history through Win+G.

What it is not

It is not a stats analyzer, win predictor or public reputation service, and it does not decide whether another player is trustworthy.

Frequently asked questions

Is it free?

Yes, the current version is free.

Which queues are supported?

Ranked, normal, ARAM and human custom games; bot matches are ignored.

Does it sync to the cloud?

No. There is currently no account or cloud sync.
