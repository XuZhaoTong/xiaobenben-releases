What are XiaoBenBen's limitations?

It supports Windows 10/11 x64 and depends on the local League Client roster. Local records do not sync across PCs, and Windows notifications may be hidden by exclusive fullscreen or Focus mode.

Updated: 2026-07-10

System and client

Only Windows x64 is supported. Without League Client, a real roster cannot be captured automatically.

Notification limits

Windows notifications are not guaranteed over exclusive fullscreen, so Win+G is the reliable way to check important prior notes.

Meaning of a note

A note is a private, subjective memory. It is not verified fact and should not be used for harassment, defamation or public targeting.

Frequently asked questions

Does it support macOS?

Not currently.

Can records sync between devices?

Not currently.

Will every player always resolve?

No. Client fields vary between champion select, loading and live game; the app merges what is available.
