Are player notes uploaded?

No. XiaoBenBen has no player-note server, account or telemetry. Riot IDs, matches and your notes stay on your Windows PC.

Updated: 2026-07-10

Local records

Records are visible only to the current Windows user. The operator cannot query what you wrote from a server.

Necessary requests

The app may contact Riot Data Dragon for public images and Microsoft Store or GitHub for software updates.

Anti-cheat boundary

It does not read game memory, inject a DLL, hook DirectX, or attempt to bypass Vanguard.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a server copy after uninstalling?

No, because notes were never uploaded.

Can people search my notes?

No.

How do I erase everything?

Close the app and delete %APPDATA%\小本本.
